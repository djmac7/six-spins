Six Spins — build the NBA GOAT in six spins

A free daily NBA puzzle — like Wordle, but for basketball. Spin six random all-time team-years, steal one rating from a star on each roster, and build a 99 OVR player in about a minute a day.

  1. Spin — land on a random all-time NBA roster (e.g. the ’96 Bulls).
  2. Steal — take one ability from one player: Shooting, Scoring, Playmaking, Defense, Rebounding, or Clutch.
  3. Build — fill all six abilities across six spins and chase a 99 OVR.
